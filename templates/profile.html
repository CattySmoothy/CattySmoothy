Monthly Prompt Playlist

Each month's prompt comes with a curated community playlist. Members submit one song that helped them through a specific emotion, creating a collaborative sonic environment for the art session. Visual art is always better with good music ><

Club Plan
  • Member Code of Care: A commitment to non-judgment, confidentiality, and "process over product"
  • Digital Archive: A dedicated platform where members can upload high-quality scans of their contributions
  • Monthly Events: Planned on Canva and posted on social media
