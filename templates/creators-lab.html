The Creator's Lab

A guided creative workshop — bring your character concept to life, one step at a time.

Step 1 — The Spark

What emotion, moment, or story inspired this character? There are no wrong answers — just honest ones.

Step 2 — Color Palette

Choose up to 5 colors that represent your character's energy. Each color adds a layer to their story.

Step 3 — Character Form

Describe the physical shape and presence of your character — how do they move through the world?

Step 4 — Character Identity

Give your character a name and a personality — who are they when no one is watching?

Step 5 — Story Arc

Every character is on a journey. What challenges do they face and how do they grow?

Step 6 — Inspiration Sources

What animals, objects, plants, or places inspired this character? Everything counts.

Step 7 — The Soundtrack

What songs does this character listen to? Share a playlist that captures their inner world.

♫

Step 8 — Reference Gallery

Upload images of your inspirations — plants, animals, objects, sketches, mood boards. Anything that helps tell the story.
